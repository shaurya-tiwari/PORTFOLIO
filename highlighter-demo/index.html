The Art of Subtle Design

When building digital experiences, we often seek ways to bring human elements onto the screen. A hand-drawn highlight effect can guide a reader's eye to the most important parts of a sentence without feeling rigid or artificial.

Highlighters in the real world aren't perfect rectangles. They have uneven edges, varying opacity, and a layered texture where the ink saturates the paper. This demo recreates that organic feel using pure CSS and a tiny bit of Vanilla JavaScript for scroll detection.

Whether you want to emphasize a key insight or just add a bit of personality to your blog, this effect stays high-performance and accessible.
